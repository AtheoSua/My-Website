United Nations | SDGs

End Poverty in all its forms everywhere

Sustainable Development Goal 1 (No Poverty) aims to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere by 2030, currently defined as living on less than $2.15 a day. It also seeks to reduce the proportion of people living in poverty in all its dimensions, ensure equal access to resources and services, and build resilience among the poor to economic, social, and environmental shocks. Achieving this goal is essential for creating a more equitable and sustainable world, as poverty is a root cause of hunger, poor health, limited education, and social instability.
